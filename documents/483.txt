Reaction products and a a-lactalbumin reference sample (50 ml) were applied to
a Superose 6 10/300 GL column (GE Healthcare, Uppsala) connected to an Akta
Purifier system at room temperature. The column was equilibrated and eluted
with 0.1 M ammonium acetate buffer pH 6.8 at a flow rate of 0.5 mL min 1
. The eluate was monitored at 280 nm.
